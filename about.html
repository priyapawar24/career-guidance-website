About Our Community Project

This Career Guidance Platform is developed as a Community Engagement Project (Consultancy Initiative) to support rural and semi-urban students in making informed career decisions after 10th and 12th.

Through this platform, we aim to provide simple explanations, career pathways, awareness about available opportunities, and confidence to students who lack proper guidance and exposure.

Project Objectives

  • To guide rural students about suitable career options.
  • To reduce confusion and misinformation about education choices.
  • To present information in simple and understandable form.
  • To support parents and first-generation learners.
